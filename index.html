Schulferien & Feiertage DE

The ultimate app to check school holidays and public holidays in Germany

Easily check school holidays and public holidays for all 16 German federal states (Bundesländer). Modern interface with visual calendars, available for iPhone and iPad.

Main Features:

  • All 16 German Bundesländer
  • Interactive calendars with marked days
  • 6 holiday periods (Winter, Easter, Pentecost, Summer, Autumn, Christmas)
  • Adaptive design for iPhone and iPad
  • Support for portrait and landscape orientation
  • Annually updated data
